  • Q: Can I hire you for photography, web development, app creation, or other projects?

    No, I've never been "for hire." While I appreciate the interest, my creative skills are personal pursuits. I highly recommend you locate a for-hire professional in the field of your need.


  • Q: What does "korze" mean?
  • A:

    "Korze" is a name I created for a character in a fantasy RPG over two decades ago. As I continued using this character across various games, the name naturally became my online alias. Despite occasional speculation about hidden meanings or codes, it's simply a name I chose, much like any other. It holds no deeper significance beyond being my long-standing online identity.
